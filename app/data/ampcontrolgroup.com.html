Our values

Ampcontrol is trusted by customers worldwide, with a proven track record of over half a century of challenging conventions and delivering smart, custom solutions and services. We champion our company’s highest standards, operating within a set of core values that allow us to lead industry and shape culture. These values include:

Health, Safety, Environment & Quality
We deliver quality and innovative products to our customers in a sustainable way, going home safe and well every day. We’re dedicated to ensuring a high level of trust and integrity not just across our solutions and services, but within our business practices.
People
We couldn’t lead industry without valuing collaboration and uplifting the people behind our company. Our people communicate and act with respect and integrity and make the best decisions for our customers and community, our business and each other.
Innovation
At Ampcontrol, we encourage radical thinking and collaboration to bring about disruptive innovations when it comes to energy. Our people are committed to challenging the status quo, partnering with customers and the world’s brightest minds to develop solutions of scale that can transform industry.
Customer
We’re a solutions provider, answering our customers’ complex challenges with agility, professionalism and pride. We listen to our customers and are committed to supporting them in the energy transition, empowering them to help make net zero a global reality.
Performance
Our people are confident in the role they play and positively represent the business in everything they do. We strive for outcomes and results that serve our customers and community, as well as our company, and are proud of our proven track record of delivering innovative solutions for over half a century.
Our People

Smart people who challenge the status quo

At Ampcontrol, we have a proud collective of thought-leaders, world-changers and innovative minds that are consistently pushing the envelope to make global net zero a reality. We’re fuelled by the communities and people we serve, developing solutions that will have a lasting impact on generations to come.

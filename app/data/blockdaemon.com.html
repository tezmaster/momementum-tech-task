Products

What is daemonOS?

daemonOS underpins the Blockdaemon product suite.

With daemonOS you get access to a seamless blockchain-platform, from high-quality nodes to easily-accessible staking infrastructure, MPC tech, and beyond.

Wallet

Blockdaemon Wallet is the digital asset wallet for institutions that delivers the benefits of self-custody along with security, liquidity, and compliance.

Learn More
Nodes

Having launched over 150K nodes, Blockdaemon provides you with access to all major blockchains. This includes dedicated nodes and/or API access to protocols, NFTs and many other specialized APIs.

Learn More
Stake

Our institutional staking solution is easily accessible through your Blockdaemon wallet or as a standalone solution via our Staking API, liquid staking, white-label and public validators.

Learn More

Revolutionize Your Web3 Services with Blockdaemon powered by daemonOS

At Blockdaemon, we’re proud to offer you a choice of the finest wallet, staking, and node services and technologies, available as individual offerings or as fully integrated systems orchestrated via daemonOS. 

You can think of daemonOS as the operating system running in the background to orchestrate your Blockdaemon powered Web3 infrastructure and more. We provide developers with the tools to build Web3 apps seamlessly, without ever worrying about infrastructure or security issues.

Benefits

Why choose Blockdaemon?

Unparalleled Security and Compliance

We meet the highest compliance standards, including ISO 27001 certification. Our dedicated compliance team ensure rigorous policies are in place. These include risk-based KYC diligence, sanctions screening, and anti-money laundering monitoring. We also assess on-chain behavioral indicators and perform risk scoring to ensure we provide a secure and compliant service.

Seamless Integration and Scalability

With daemonOS, you can seamlessly integrate Blockdaemon solutions with your existing infrastructure. This reduces your overhead and promotes rapid innovation. Our infrastructure is scalable and redundant, with 99.9% uptime and full node management by our expert team of engineers.

Dedicated Customer
Support

At Blockdaemon, we provide the best possible customer support. Our dedicated team provides <24 hour response time for enterprise clients, ensuring that you get the help you need when you need it.

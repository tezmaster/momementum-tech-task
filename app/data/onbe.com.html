Transform costs into profits

Increase ROI

Transform payments from a cost center to a profit center with payment solutions designed to reduce administrative expenses and maximize revenue. From driving spend-back to winning back exiting customers, you'll unlock opportunities to optimize your program.

Deliver Choice

Meet your recipients where they are - anywhere in the world. We provide the platform, expertise and tools to help you deliver modern payment experiences with popular choices such as digital wallets, real-time payments and global bank transfers.

Reduce Complexity

Stay agile and scale your disbursement operation quickly and seamlessly. We offer no-code, low-code and easy API integrations to get you to market quickly and relieve your team of cumbersome processes, so you can focus on what you do best: growing your business.

Remove Risk

Our solutions are compliant by design. We take on everything from escheat management to fraud monitoring to the most complex compliance issues, so you can rest assured that your business and recipients are protected.

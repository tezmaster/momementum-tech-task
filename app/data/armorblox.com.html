Security Made Simple.

Automatically protect your organization from ever-changing cybersecurity threats, such as targeted email attacks, financial fraud, data loss, and more.

Discover Product Capabilities

Stop targeted email threats

Prevent malicious email attacks that bypass legacy security controls, such as 0-day attacks, payroll fraud, vendor and account compromise, spear phishing, and impersonation threats. Armorblox uses Natural Language Understanding (NLU) and advanced machine learning models to analyze thousands of signals, including user identity and behavior, and the content and context of communications, to protect against threats that target your human layer.

Learn More About Business Email Compromise (BEC)

Prevent account compromise attempts

Stop email account compromise attempts before bad actors steal credentials, sensitive data, or launch lateral attacks. Quickly identify anomalous signals to prevent compromise attempts, and protect your organization and end users from takeover attacks that escape detection by legacy security tools.

Learn More About Email Account Compromise

Keep sensitive data out of the wrong hands

Ensure compliance by safeguarding against the accidental or intentional release of sensitive data via email. Our language models can identify sensitive information such as personal identification information (PII), payment card information (PCI), personal health information (PHI), and passwords that have been shared with unauthorized individuals, providing you with a clear assessment of data exposure risk.

Learn More About Email Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

Reduce phishing response times

Boost your email security by integrating Armorblox with your abuse mailbox to automatically identify and address user-reported email threats. When you automate security tasks, you enable your team to focus on proactive security measures that improve security processes, like searching for threats and conducting simulated attacks.

Learn More About Abuse Mailbox Remediation

Stop unwanted mail

Prevent annoying interruptions from flowing into end users' inboxes with automatic classification of graymail types, eliminating the need for manual sorting and remediation. Accurately identify legitimate email communications from malicious threats and recon email attacks that bypass legacy security layers undetected.

Learn More About Graymail Classification and Remediation

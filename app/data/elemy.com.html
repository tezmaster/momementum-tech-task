How it works

Every child is different. And so is every care journey. But our process is always consistent.

We spend time understanding your child’s history and concerns. Your care team reviews this before you first meet, so no time is wasted.

If your child does not yet have a diagnosis, our psychiatric experts will perform an extensive evaluation to make an accurate diagnosis.

If your child needs therapy, our clinical experts will build a personalized, at-home care plan tailored to your child’s unique needs.
